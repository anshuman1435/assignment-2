Double fudgy Chocolate cake

he most amazing, one bowl, best fudgy chocolate cake is so rich and decadent, with the perfect balance of fluffy and fudgy!

ingredients :
all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, 1 teaspoon salt
vegetable oil or melted butter, 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, 3/4 cup hot water or brewed coffee ,powdered sugar, unsweetened cocoa powder, milk or heavy cream, vanilla extract

RECIPE INDEX  ABOUT ME BOOKS CONTACT ME

recipe :
Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
Mix dry ingredients: flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
Add eggs, milk, oil or butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
Gradually stir in hot water or coffee until smooth batter forms.
Pour batter into pans and bake for 25-30 minutes.
For frosting, beat butter, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, milk, vanilla, and salt until creamy.
Frost cooled cakes, stack, and frost again.
Decorate with toppings as desired. Enjoy your chocolate fudgy cake!

World of Taste" began as a passion project by a group of food enthusiasts who shared a common love for cooking, cultural exploration, and community building. Inspired by the diverse culinary traditions found across the globe.
Cultural Celebration: Our primary goal is to celebrate the rich tapestry of global cuisines by providing a platform for people to share authentic recipes, culinary traditions, and stories from their heritage.
Community Engagement: We aim to foster a vibrant and inclusive community where food lovers can connect, interact, and collaborate with one another. Through shared experiences and mutual appreciation for food, we strive to build lasting friendships and cultural understanding.
